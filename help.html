HELP CENTER

How do I start the quiz?

Click the Start Quiz button on the home page

How many questions are there?

There are 10 multiple-choice questions

How much time do I get?

Each question has a 30-second timer

Can I go back to a previous question?

Yes, use the Previous button to review earlier questions

Is my progress saved?

Yes, your answers and progress are saved automatically

How is my score calculated?

You earn points for each correct answer. Your final score is displayed at the end of the quiz
